About Me

I'm an undergraduate double-majoring in Information Science and Graphic Design with a certificate in Digital Studies at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My expected graduation date is May 2027. With my experience in Information Science (understanding how people organize, find, and use information) and Graphic Design (communicating effectively through visual systems), I'm passionate about applying this unique lens to create digital experiences that are visually compelling.

I strive to create more digital mediums that blend aesthetics with functional designs. My work is characterized by cohesive designs, smooth animations, and thoughtful user interactions.
