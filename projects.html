My Design Process

1

Research & Discovery

I conducted interviews with 20 potential users to understand their diet habits. Key insights revealed that people wanted simple recipes that they could make easily each day, or even meals that could be eaten throughout the week.

2

User Personas & Journey

Created a primary user persona, which was a typical college student who lives on campus, and they struggle with cooking daily meals or finding ideas of what to eat next. They look for simple and affordable recipes they can make while uplifting their mood and energy for the day.

3

Wireframing & Prototyping

Developed mid to high-fidelity wireframes focusing on the look in digital tools like Figma for more refined elements, clearer text/images.

Low-Fidelity Mid-Fidelity High-Fidelity
4

Visual Design System

Developed a cohesive design system with a soft pastel color palette, dream-inspired icons, and smooth micro-interactions.

5

Testing & Iteration

Conducted usability tests with participants. Made iterations based on feedback, including simplifying the process and adding functionality.
